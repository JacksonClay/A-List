The Stoner's Cafe'

Meet The Dj of light. You have probably already heard or seen him if you have not had the chance to personally meet him yet. You can usually catch him around Monti just Vibin. Usually, it will bring quite the gathering.

The Dj of Light is the founder of The Stoner's Cafe' where he has kept the vibes going for some time now. On or off the islands you can be sure to find a good time amongst the members of the groups members. Be sure to ask how to join or visit The Stoner's Cafe'.

Don't miss out on The DJ's Epic parties! Be sure to ask DJ about the stoner cafe and if you can possibly become a member!

You will likely hear the Party Bus before you see it! Be sure to follow the music and join the party, even if you have to follow in your own vehicle!

If you see all the wrapped vehicles be sure to hit up Monti Motors, the A-List, or Mike Lowry to rent you one! They go fast!
